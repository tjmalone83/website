Where Aureus Excels

Tackling complexity
across the family structure

Aureus is specifically designed to address some of the most demanding challenges facing affluent families — from sitting atop the family structure to navigating private businesses and multi-generational transitions.

Financial Reporting

As wealth grows, families need a trusted set of eyes across their entire portfolio. We bring discipline and clarity to consolidated financial reporting — so you always know where you stand.

Real Estate

Whether commercial or residential, we help families manage growing property portfolios with the same rigor applied to financial assets — from acquisition through disposition.

Investment Oversight

We work closely with your wealth advisors and asset managers to help the family truly understand their investment portfolio and evaluate risk with confidence.*

Private Trustee

Each family determines their preference for trustee arrangements. We serve as private trustee or co-trustee, placing the family's interests at the center of every decision.

Family Management

Affluent families span two to three generations — each with different needs and aspirations. We provide guidance, structure, and continuity across the entire family.

Estate Planning

Design, planning, execution, and ongoing management of the estate plan often spans years of careful work — and provides some of the greatest long-term value to the family.

Managing Partner

tom.malone@aureus-fo.com 610.390.9527 LinkedIn

After eighteen years at a top-ten public accounting firm, Tom formed Aureus Family Office alongside two founding families. During his tenure in client service, he was responsible for tax compliance and strategy, estate planning, and navigating the complexities inherent in family business structures across multiple generations.

Tom grew up in the family business world — working for his grandfather Sam at the family's Italian bakery. That experience gave him an early and lasting understanding of what it means to build something across generations, and what it truly costs to protect it.

He is a licensed Certified Public Accountant in Pennsylvania and earned his professional coaching credentials through iPEC Coaching. Tom currently resides in Allentown, PA.
